# Public Intake Link Plan

Each business workspace should eventually receive a public customer quote intake link.

## Public Customer Link

Example:

```text
https://demo.stanleysync.app/intake/company-name
```

This page is for the end customer. It should not require login.

Customers can:

- Open the link.
- Answer guided quote intake questions.
- Submit a quote request.

The submitted quote appears inside StanleySync for the business owner or team to review.

## Business Owner Login

Owner/admin login is separate:

```text
https://demo.stanleysync.app/login
```

Business users can:

- Review quote requests.
- Convert quotes to jobs.
- Create invoices.
- Export PDFs.

## Future Improvements

- Custom company slug in workspace settings.
- Branded intake pages per business.
- Public link copy button.
- Intake view analytics.
- Customer quote approval portal.
