# StanleySync Lead Engine

Lead Engine is a planned future module in the StanleySync suite.

## Purpose

Lead Engine will eventually support top-of-funnel lead capture, qualification, and sales-system support that sits upstream of QuoteFlow.

## Planned Features

- lead intake and categorization
- qualification workflow
- campaign/source tracking
- pipeline support
- handoff into QuoteFlow when a lead becomes a real quote request

## Relationship to QuoteFlow

Lead Engine is not part of the current QuoteFlow runtime.

Planned connection:

1. Lead Engine captures and qualifies demand
2. qualified opportunities hand off into QuoteFlow
3. QuoteFlow handles customer intake and quoting

## Current State

This folder is a placeholder only. There is no runnable Lead Engine app on this machine yet.
